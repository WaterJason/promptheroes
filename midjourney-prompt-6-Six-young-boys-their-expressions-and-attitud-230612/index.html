Midjourney Prompt

Six young boys, their expressions and attitudes captured with precision, stand in a circle around an object that is difficult to identify, but which is certainly the reason behind their discussion. Their well-worn clothes indicate that they are from a working class area - the wooden fence, the graffiti and the torn posters reinforce this impression – while their smocks indicate that they are schoolboys: we are in the early 1880s and they stand outside on a cobbled street on a breezy afternoon

Midjourney Prompt 翻译

六个小男孩，他们的表情和态度被准确地捕捉到，站在一个很难辨认的物体周围，但这肯定是他们讨论的原因。他们破旧的衣服表明他们来自工人阶级地区–木栅栏、涂鸦和撕破的海报强化了这种印象–而他们的工作服表明他们是学生：我们生活在19世纪80年代初，在一个微风习习的下午，他们站在外面的鹅卵石街道上。
