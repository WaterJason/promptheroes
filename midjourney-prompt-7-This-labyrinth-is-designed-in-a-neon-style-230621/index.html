Midjourney Prompt

This labyrinth is designed in a neon style and has a three-dimensional form. It is executed in shades of blue-green, which create a sense of depth and volume. The labyrinth does not contain people and represents a complex system of corridors and turns that create the impression of continuity and infinity. The view of the labyrinth is presented from above, and you can see all its rectangular faces. The walls of the labyrinth are made in a modern style, with smooth surfaces and reflective light. This creates the effect of mirror surfaces that reflect the surrounding space and make the labyrinth even more deep and voluminous. The resolution of the labyrinth is high, and you can see every detail. The labyrinth is bright and natural, mainly executed in blue-green tones, but also contains some other bright colors that create contrast and an interesting visual effect. Overall, this labyrinth represents a unique combination of modern design and technological possibilities that create an impressive effect of volume and depth.

Midjourney Prompt 翻译

这座迷宫采用霓虹灯风格设计，具有三维造型。它是在蓝绿色的阴影中执行的，这创造了一种深度和体量的感觉。迷宫没有人，而是一个复杂的走廊和转弯系统，给人一种连续性和无边无际的印象。迷宫的视图是从上面呈现的，你可以看到它的所有矩形面。迷宫的墙壁采用现代风格，表面光滑，光线反光。这创造了镜面的效果，反射了周围的空间，使迷宫变得更深更大。迷宫的分辨率很高，你可以看到每一个细节。迷宫明亮自然，主要采用蓝绿色色调，但也包含一些其他明亮的颜色，以创造对比和有趣的视觉效果。总体而言，这个迷宫代表了现代设计和技术可能性的独特结合，创造了令人印象深刻的体积和深度效果。
